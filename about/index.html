About

I am Arman Arisman, an architect, design technologist, and researcher based in Indonesia.

My work explores the relationship between architecture, computation, and design cognition, with a particular interest in how digital tools influence architectural ideas, decisions, and the way architects think.

My research focuses on representation, creative exploration, cognitive load, and decision-making in digital design environments.
